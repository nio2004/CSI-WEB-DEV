Hangman Game

Welcome to Hangman Game made using JavaScript

Game must be played in console of your browser.

You are given a word to guess.
Word is name of languages.
You are given 5 lives to guess the correct word.
The input must be a single letter, either uppercase (A-Z) or lowercase (a-z).
Each time you guess a letter wrong, your life will be decrement by one.

Best of luck !!!!
